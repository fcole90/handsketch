HandSketch is a prototype for a drawing application, which uses hand gesture tracking as input source for the selection of drawing tools, colors, and their options.

This is the final project submitted for the Human-Computer Interaction Class 2015/2016.
By Martino Pilia, Fabio Colella, Michele Contu, and Hicham Lafhouli.

Selected as one of the three Best and exemplary HCI projects of the academic year 2015/2016
